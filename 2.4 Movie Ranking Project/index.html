My top3 movies of all time

1. The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 American drama film written and directed by Frank Darabont, based on the 1982 novella Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption by Stephen King. It stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red"

2. The Godfather

The Godfather is a 1972 American crime film directed by Francis Ford Coppola

3. The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed, produced, and co-written by Christopher Nolan. Based on the DC Comics character Batman, the film is the second installment of Nolan's The Dark Knight Trilogy and a sequel to 2005's Batman Begins, starring Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman.
